1. What does "kaikaku" mean?
"Kaikaku" is a Japanese term that translates to "reform" or "innovation." It is often used in the context of business and management to refer to a radical change or transformation in processes, systems, or strategies to achieve significant improvements or breakthrough results. Kaikaku emphasizes a disruptive and revolutionary approach to change, as opposed to incremental improvements.
